Gadgets Galaxy — Payment Terms & Policies

Secure and transparent payment procedures for our customers.

Last updated: October 10, 2025

1. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept major credit/debit cards, UPI, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for eligible locations.

2. Payment Security

All online payments are processed through encrypted and secure gateways. Gadgets Galaxy does not store full card or banking details on its servers.

3. Refund & Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation: Orders can be canceled before shipment for a full refund.
  • Refunds: If products are defective or not as described, refunds are processed within 7–10 business days after inspection.
  • Partial Refunds: May apply if packaging is opened or accessories are missing.

4. Cash on Delivery

COD is available for select locations and order amounts. Please prepare the exact amount as mentioned in your invoice.

5. Payment Disputes

For any payment or refund-related issues, please contact our support team with your order ID. We aim to resolve all disputes within 7 business days.

Note: Policies may be updated periodically to meet new security or business requirements.
