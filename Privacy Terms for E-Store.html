Gadgets Galaxy — Privacy Policy

How we handle your data securely and respectfully.

Last updated: October 10, 2025

At Gadgets Galaxy, your privacy is very important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information: name, email, phone, and shipping address when you create an account or place an order.
  • Order information: products purchased, payment method, and transaction details.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and site usage analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To provide customer support and updates.
  • To send promotional offers (only if you opt in).
  • To improve website performance and user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data. Limited details may be shared with trusted partners (payment gateways, delivery services) strictly for completing your orders.

4. Security Measures

We use SSL encryption and secure systems to safeguard your information. Although no online system is fully risk-free, we continuously monitor and update our protections.

5. Your Rights

  • Access or update your account information anytime.
  • Opt out of marketing emails via the unsubscribe link.
  • Request account deletion by contacting us.
